Grade 7 Online Registration

Parents and guardians of students placed at New Amsterdam Secondary School may now complete the Grade 7 registration process online.

Before You Begin

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for upload:

  • Passport-size Photograph
  • NGSA Result Slip
  • Birth Certificate
  • School Medical Record ("Big Clinic Card")

Registration Fee

A G$5,000 Registration Fee must be paid to:

Republic Bank Limited
Account Number: 97575406689

The original Republic Bank deposit slip must be presented on Orientation Day.
