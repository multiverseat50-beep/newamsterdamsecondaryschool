About Our School

Discover the identity, values and educational purpose of New Amsterdam Secondary School.

Welcome to NASS

New Amsterdam Secondary School serves students through a broad programme of academic, technical, vocational and co-curricular education. The school promotes high expectations, disciplined effort, mutual respect and meaningful cooperation.

Our community is guided by the belief that every learner should have access to opportunities that develop knowledge, skills, character and confidence.

The MultiVerse of Opportunities

This identity reflects the many pathways available to students: academics, technical education, vocational training, sport, leadership, agriculture, culture and service.

Mission

To provide a supportive, inclusive and high-quality education that prepares students for further study, employment, entrepreneurship and responsible citizenship.

Vision

To be a leading secondary school recognised for academic excellence, innovation, technical competence, strong values and community impact.

Motto

Progress Through Co-Operation

Advancement is achieved when the entire school community works together.
