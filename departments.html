Our Departments

Our academic, technical and vocational departments work collaboratively to provide students with a broad, balanced and relevant secondary education.

Learning Across the MultiVerse

Each department contributes to the development of students' knowledge, practical skills, character, creativity and confidence. Through classroom instruction, practical activities, assessment, enrichment and co-curricular opportunities, our departments prepare students for further education, employment, entrepreneurship and responsible citizenship.

📚

Department of English

The Department of English develops effective communication, critical thinking, creativity and an appreciation of language and literature. Students build competence in reading, writing, speaking and listening.

Subjects and Areas

  • English A
  • English B
  • Communication Studies
  • Literatures in English

Student Activities

  • Debating and public speaking
  • Poetry and dramatic presentations
  • Reading and writing competitions
  • Examination preparation sessions
📐

Department of Mathematics

The Mathematics Department develops logical reasoning, accuracy, problem-solving and analytical thinking. Students are encouraged to apply mathematical concepts to everyday, scientific and technical situations.

Subjects and Areas

  • Mathematics
  • Additional Mathematics
  • Pure Mathematics
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Integrated Mathematics

Student Activities

  • Mathematics quizzes
  • Problem-solving challenges
  • Remedial and enrichment sessions
  • Math Fair
🔬

Department of Science

The Science Department promotes curiosity, investigation and evidence-based reasoning. Students explore the natural and physical world through scientific inquiry, laboratory activities and practical applications.

Subjects Offered

  • Integrated Science
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Environmental Science
  • Human and Social Biology

Student Activities

  • Laboratory investigations
  • Science projects and demonstrations
  • STEM-related activities
  • Science Fair
  • Field-based learning
🌍

Department of Social Studies

The Social Studies Department helps students understand society, history, geography, citizenship and human relationships. Students develop research, interpretation, decision-making and civic responsibility.

Subjects Offered

  • Social Studies
  • History
  • Geography
  • Sociology
  • Law
  • Caribbean Studies

Student Activities

  • Research projects
  • Field trips and community studies
  • Current affairs discussions
  • Civic and cultural activities

  • Principles of Business
  • Office Administration
  • Logistics and Chain Supply Management

  • Entrepreneurship

Student Activities

  • Financial literacy activities

The Information Technology Department develops digital literacy, computational thinking and practical computer skills. Students learn to use technology safely, responsibly and productively.

Subjects and Areas

  • Information Technology
  • Digital Literacy

  • Electronic Document Preparation and Management (EDPM)
  • Computer Science

Student Activities

  • Practical computer assignments
  • Digital presentations and projects
  • Data-processing activities
  • Technology-based problem-solving
  • Coding

Department of Industrial Technology

The Department of Industrial Technology provides practical, technical and competency-based education. Students develop design, problem-solving, construction, fabrication, electrical, drawing and workshop skills.

  • Furniture Making

  • Design Technology
