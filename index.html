About NASS

Preparing students for a changing world

We provide a broad secondary education supported by academic, technical, vocational, leadership and co-curricular opportunities.

Our students are encouraged to think critically, work collaboratively, serve responsibly and pursue excellence in every field. Through CSEC, CAPE, CVQ and CCSLC pathways, learners can build strong foundations for further education, employment and entrepreneurship.
